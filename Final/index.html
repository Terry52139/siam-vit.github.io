About Us

SIAM stands for Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics. It is one of the most prestigious community where enthusiasts of mathematics and its applied applications can contribute, nurture and enhance their interests. Applied mathematics, in partnership with computational science is essential in solving many real world problems.Our mission is to build cooperation between mathematics and the world of science and technology through publications, research and community through membership activities, publications of journals, books and conferences. For further details, you can go to SIAM website.

Our Events




Riviera-VGT

VIT'S Got Talent is one of the biggest hits of Riviera, the cultural fest of VIT Vellore. Here, a wide range of participants get a platform to showcase their various hidden talents like dancing, singing, acting, beatboxing, etc in front of an audience and judges. The best of which are awarded with cash prizes which can range upto 15,000 rupees.

Gravitas

Gravitas is an international fest organised by VIT, Vellore usually in the month of September/October. The technology oriented fest which continues for three days, provides the students a platform to showcase their talents. Students from different colleges come here to take part in the competitions organised by various chapters.

Hackulus

Hackulus is a 24hr hackathon organised by SIAM. The event has multiple tracks and expects to see participants from all over India. It aims to spotlight innovative ideas to real world problems with constant mentorship and guidance by experts in domains such as Web Dev, App Dev and Machine Learning. It also gives a chance to beginners by providing a separate beginners round.

Online Events

Apart from our various events our youtube page is also constantly updated with the latest in technology and talks with industry experts. Our various playlists aim to help both students and professionals with everyday work, further our team is constantly making new crash course videos for students to get a comprehensive revision for the end of semester exams.
